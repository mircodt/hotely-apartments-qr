FAQ

Things hosts ask before paying

What exactly do I get for $19.99/year? +

Per apartment, you get four things: (1) instant access to the full step-by-step video tutorial (8 chapters, ~15 min), (2) a custom physical QR cavaliere with your logo printed on it, shipped to your door, (3) 1 full year of Hotely service (Wi-Fi, check-in, restaurants, experiences, app, support), and (4) auto-renewal at the same $19.99/year — cancel any time, no commitment. Shipping is added at checkout based on your address and number of apartments.

How is shipping calculated? +

Shipping is per apartment (one cavaliere per address) and is calculated automatically at checkout based on your shipping country. EU usually €3–5, UK/US/CA usually $5–8, rest of world a bit more. We use trackable mail with insurance.

Why do I have to enter a shipping address and upload a logo? +

Because we ship you the physical cavaliere (the one you saw in the reel) — and we print your logo on it, right next to the QR code. So at checkout we ask for two things: your shipping address and your logo file (PNG, SVG or PDF — we'll auto-clean the background for you). If you don't have a logo yet, you can skip the upload and we'll ship the cavaliere with just the Hotely.ai branding.

When do I receive the tutorial vs. the cavaliere? +

The video tutorial unlocks instantly after payment — we email you a magic link and you land on your dashboard with the video ready. The physical cavaliere is printed with your logo within 24h and arrives at your door in 4–8 business days (sometimes faster in the EU).

Can I cancel anytime? +

Yes — cancel any time, in one click from the Hotely app. There is no minimum commitment. The subscription won't renew the following year and the cavaliere stays yours to keep.

Is the first $19.99 refundable? +

No. Because the $19.99 also pays for the physical cavaliere — which we print with your logo and ship to you immediately — the initial payment (and the shipping fee) is non-refundable. The good news: the cavaliere is yours to keep forever, even if you cancel before the next yearly renewal.

How long does setup actually take? +

About 15 minutes if you follow along with the video. The longest step is typing in your favorite local spots — and you can skip that and let our AI seed it for you.

Do I need any tech skills? +

No. The tutorial is built for hosts, not developers. If you can use Instagram, you can use Hotely.

What if I have multiple apartments? +

Each apartment is $19.99/year and gets its own QR cavaliere + page (shipping per cavaliere). Volume discounts kick in at 5+ apartments — email mircodallatorre@hotely.ai for a custom quote.
